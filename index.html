Xiaobao Wang 王晓宝


Ph.D & Assistant Professor

Xiaobao Wang (王晓宝) is currently an assistant professor in Tianjin University (TJU), China. He received BA, ME, and Ph.D degree from Tianjin University. His supervisors were Prof. Jianwu Dang (党建武) and Prof. Di Jin (金弟). His research interests include graph neural networks, complex network analysis, social network analysis, intent understanding, and rumor detection. He has published many papers in highly regarded journals and conferences, such as AAAI, WWW, IJCAI, IEEE TKDE, etc.

Call for graduate and undergraduate students! I hope you are dedicated, determined and have basic programming and math skills. I also hope you can enjoy learning and life. Please feel free to contact me via email.
